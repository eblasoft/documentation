## Overview

**Ebla AI** supports Ollama, Gemini, Anthropic, OpenRouter, and OpenAI — bringing the full power of AI to your EspoCRM.

Ebla AI provides a comprehensive range of AI-driven features including smart email composition, record chat, thread analysis, field generation, formula automation, and much more.
